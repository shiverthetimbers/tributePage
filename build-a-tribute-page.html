Some life events:
1929 - Glock was born in Vienna, Austria on 19 July. He graduated from school as an engineer and joined a hand drill company.
1958 - He married Helga Glock. They were married until 2011.
1963 - He and Helga co-founded the family business.
1981 - Glock applied to patent his first and most famous handgun, the Glock 17.
1999 - Glock survived an attemped assassination by a french mercenary hired by his business partner. Both the partner and would-be assassin were convicted of attempted murder.
2023 - Glock died on 27 December.
